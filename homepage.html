Our Passion for Petals

DFlowershop was founded in 2020 by Dana Chen with a simple belief: that flowers should bring **joy, not stress**. We are committed to redefining the floral industry by focusing on sustainability, quality, and exceptional design.

We partner directly with local, ethical growers to ensure the freshest, most vibrant blooms make it into your arrangements. This commitment to farm-to-door delivery means your flowers last longer and support our local community.
